# Limitations and Assumptions

## General assumptions

- The repository uses simplified financial models.
- The objective is validation evidence, not trading advice.
- Examples use public or illustrative data.
- The project avoids proprietary models, confidential data and institutional workflows.

## Current limitations

- Black-Scholes assumes constant volatility and continuous trading.
- Historical VaR assumes the past loss distribution is informative for current risk.
- Stress scenarios are simplified and should not be interpreted as full regulatory stress tests.
- Monitoring outputs are examples of validation logic, not production risk reports.
