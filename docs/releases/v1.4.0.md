# Quant Model Risk Lab v1.4.0

**Release status:** `RELEASED_WITH_MONITORING`  
**Validated collected tests:** `708`  
**Required CI:** `Python 3.12 validation`

v1.4.0 extends the public XVA validation platform into a governed portfolio-scale model-risk laboratory with multi-currency exposure, initial-margin and capital proxies, MVA and KVA, incremental and allocation analytics, operational recalculation, independent challenge, lifecycle governance, and advisory-only GenAI evidence review.

## Principal additions

- canonical portfolio ingestion, lineage, and deterministic hashes;
- multi-currency exposure, collateral remuneration, triangulation, and attribution;
- transparent IM proxies and separately attributable MVA;
- transparent public capital proxies and KVA;
- full-revaluation incremental XVA, marginal convergence, Euler validity, and allocation residuals;
- dependency-aware recalculation, deterministic caches, checkpoints, chunking, parallel determinism, and scale evidence;
- unified challengers, stability, drift, remediation governance, lifecycle dashboarding, and governed GenAI challenge.

## Monitoring and boundaries

The release remains under monitoring for challenger disagreement, stability, drift, operational budgets, and evidence freshness. It does not grant production approval, regulatory approval, legal enforceability, institution-specific calibration, or investment authority.

The `v1.3.0` release tag and evidence remain preserved and immutable.
